Create a brutally memorable book cover for "Eu" by Augusto dos Anjos.

Visual family: obvious, feverish, psychedelic expressionist painting, almost Van Gogh pushed into rot and flesh, intentionally a little ugly in a magnetic commercial way.
Central scene: a tormented man in a pale suit clutching his chest while his body and the air around him dissolve into swirling organic matter, raw red flowers, exposed nerves, roots, worms, stars, viscera-like textures and cosmic decay. The whole image should feel like poetry turning into meat, hallucination, disease, and revelation at once.
Palette: dominant visceral reds, raw crimson, inflamed orange, bruised wine, rotting pink, dirty cream, and small poisonous green accents.
Lighting: violent inner glow, hot painterly turbulence, unstable and luminous, as if the canvas itself has a fever.
Framing: vertical 2:3 composition, central figure dominating the cover, heavy swirling brushwork, high-impact silhouette, immediate thumbnail shock value.
Mood: grotesque, ecstatic, wounded, visionary, nauseatingly beautiful.
Character emotion: intense, feverish, proud, half-collapsing, staring like someone who has seen too much matter and too much death.
Texture and finish: thick impasto, visible brush turbulence, distorted organic forms, loud and unforgettable, selling danger rather than elegance.

Avoid: tasteful restraint, decorative border, polite literary beauty, clean symmetry, safe composition, muted teal palette, delicate ornament, generic gothic darkness, minimalist design.
