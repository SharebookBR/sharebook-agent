Book cover, original authorial art for the novel "Os Maias" by Eca de Queiros. Visual direction: art nouveau decorative poster, not photorealistic, not dark, not gloomy. Central scene: an elegant Lisbon boulevard in the late 19th century, with a refined couple in full figure near a carriage, framed by a grand townhouse facade, flowering balcony, and subtle emotional tension in their posture. Palette: warm ivory, emerald green, old rose, deep burgundy, muted gold accents, with a crisp touch of porcelain blue as the color break. Lighting: luminous late-afternoon glow, polished and airy, high contrast but graceful, no smoke, no storm, no night scene. Framing: vertical book-cover composition, medium-wide shot, full figures clearly readable, architectural backdrop supporting the drama without swallowing the characters. The image should feel seductive, elegant, emotionally charged, and instantly legible as a classic of desire, status, and decay. Avoid: dark cover, teal-and-orange look, sepia woodcut, generic silhouettes, cropped backs or shoulders, lonely figure in shadow, heavy fog, oppressive palette, cluttered background, accidental horror mood.
